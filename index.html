Homicides in the United States

Exploring murders and firearm background checks in United States from 2008 to 2014

Today there are more guns than people in the United States and over 68 percent of homicides result from firearms. This map looks at metropolitan cities that had a high numbers of homicides from 2008-2014.

This map also includes an overlay of the amount of federal firearm background checks that were conducted in 2015 by each state. This comparison can help determine if background checks for guns are in fact helping reduce the amount of homicides occurring throughout cities the United States.
